FAQs

Program Eligibility and Suitability

Who is eligible for the placement guarantee program?

We provide guaranteed placements for tech roles. This course is suitable for all 3rd year B.Tech./B.E. students and all engineering students graduating in 2023, who are interested in tech placements.

Can the program be completed along with college?

Yes. This course is only open for college students and specifically designed for them only. We have arranged the class timings such that they don't hamper with your college schedule and don't affect your performance in the college. We will also provide preparatory holidays for your semester examinations. Our curriculum will also help you in performing better in computer-related subjects in your college. Our mentors will be readily available to help you out in creating a friendly schedule for managing both college and Placewit easily.

I am experienced. Will this program be beneficial for me?

Our course is designed to help every student in improving technical skills. We will start from the basics and will cover all advanced topics like DP, Backtracking, Segment Trees, Game Theory, DP on trees, etc., so that you can see significant improvement in your skillset after completing this program, no matter where you stand today. Our batch size is less than 25. So, our mentors and instructors will give personalized attention to you, to make sure you are provided with tasks and homework according to your current level, and will take your mock interviews accordingly.

How many students will be there in my batch?

We keep our batch size less than 25 students so that we can provide personalized attention to every student and address their problems efficiently. In our last year batches also, every batch had 16 to 17 students.

Course Curriculum, Schedule, and Timings

What is the duration of the placement guarantee program?

The duration of teaching is from 8 to 10 months. But our program ends only when every student of the batch gets placed at a company they like and deserve to be at. We will keep helping and guiding you during your whole placement season, by providing company-specific tips and mock interviews.

What will be covered in placement guarantee program?

This course will cover everything that will be needed to excel in tech placements. We will cover all DS & Algorithms, Competitive Coding, Web Development, Machine Learning, Augmented Reality, Industry-level Projects, CS Subjects, Resume Enhancement, Weekly Mock Interviews, Mentorship Sessions, etc. Apart from the above learnings, you will also get career counseling & referrals in top tech companies. We will stay in touch with you to ensure that your career is successful, even after the classes have ended and you are placed. Click here to see the complete curriculum.

Will you cover any projects as a part of this program?

We will give the students a choice between Web Development + Augmented Reality, or Machine Learning. We will start from the basics and finish 5-6 good projects in live classes, in the domain that the student has chosen. All the projects will be good enough to put in your resume. The project ideas will be chosen with respect to the latest trends, so that their resume stands out from the competition. There will be multiple resume reviews to improve every single word of your resume until it is perfect. If any student wants to do any extra project in any other technology, our mentors will be there to guide and help you.

What are the class timings for placement guarantee program?

On Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday, classes will be after college hours, i.e., 6:15 pm to 8:15 pm. Thursdays and Fridays are off. On Saturday and Sunday, classes will be from 11:00 am to 4:00 pm with 1 hour lunch break. However, mentors and instructors will be available for your help after the classes also. We will conduct the classes online. You will be provided access to Placewit platform, from where you can access the classes, coding questions, study material, interview experiences, projects, and all the resources curated for your success in placements.

At the end of this program, will you provide any program completion certificate?

Yes, after completion of this program, you will receive an industrial training certificate from Placewit.

How do I get my doubts resolved?

Our instructors and mentors will be available after classes to resolve your doubts. You can also contact your instructors and mentors anytime, if you need any help or guidance. And apart from this, we also organise scheduled doubt classes. We move on to the next topic only after making sure that your understanding of the previous topic is strong.

Postpaid Fee Payment Plans

What are the payment options?

We offer a postpaid payment plan with a minimum job guarantee. It means you don’t have to pay anything to study at Placewit, and your fee payment starts only after you get a job and start earning. Also, if you are placed at a package of below 10L, you have to pay 0 fee and you can study entirely for free. If you are placed at a package from 10L to 25L, your fee will be ₹6249 paid monthly for 20 months (₹1,24,980 total), which you have to pay when you start earning. If you are placed at a CTC of 25L and above, your fee will be ₹9899 paid monthly for 20 months (₹1,74,980 total), which you have to pay when you start earning.

What is the meaning of no upfront cost?

You don’t have to pay any fee to join Placewit. You can study for free and get your guaranteed dream placement. Only after you start your job and start earning, you have to pay the program fee. And if your package is less than 10L, you don’t have to pay any fee.

How does postpaid payment work?

So, to ensure that everything is smooth for the students financially, there are 2 legal agreements. There is nothing complicated in those agreements. One is from the student to Placewit mentioning about postpaid payment plans. Another one is from Placewit to the student mentioning that students don’t have to pay the fee if their placement is below 10L. You will be provided both these agreements before final admission. If you have any queries, our team will help you resolve them.

What CTC can I expect to get after taking this program?

Placewit teaches you with a battle-tested curriculum for placements. We have a 100% placement rate with the highest salary of 45L for freshers and an average salary of 23.41L. If you are consistent at Placewit, we expect you to get placed at a package of more than 25L.

Does Placewit provide scholarships on fee payment plans?

Placewit largely supports Women In Tech. We provide ₹25,000 discount on all payment plans to our female students. Apart from that, we provide extra discounts to all our promising students.

What if I opt for the postpaid plan and don't get a job offer?

In case you can't get a job through our referrals and campus placements and remain unemployed, you don't need to pay us anything. It will be highly unlikely that you won't get a job after taking this course. And if that happens and you are not satisfied with us, you can ask for ₹20,000* from Placewit.

Does my fee increase as my salary grows?

No, your appraisals or salary hikes based on your job performance or switching the company later don't affect the fee that you pay us monthly. Your fee will depend only on the placement you get before you graduate out of college.

What if I get placed in a company myself or through any other source apart from Placewit?

Job is the end-result we all are working on together. We all will be working hard to upskill you and prepare you for your selection process. Even if you get a job through other channels, it will be considered a job only, and all the conditions for fee payment will be unchanged. You can also ask us for referrals in case you are not satisfied with your current placement.
