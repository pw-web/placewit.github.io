FAQ

Course Curriculum and Timings

Who is eligible for this course?

This course focusses only on technical jobs. 2nd and 3rd year engineering students, who are going to sit for internships or placements are eligible for this course. Other students who want a quickly replenish their coding skills can also join.

What is the duration of this course?

This course will cover all domains of computer science that will be needed to excel in a technical interview. We will cover all the topics in 3 to 4 months. If any student needs further guidance, we will provide mentorship and career counseling after the course also.

What are the timings of this course?

On weekdays, classes will be after college hours, i.e., 6:15 pm to 8:15 pm. On weekends, classes will be from 11:00 am to 4:00 pm. However, mentors and instructors will be available for the help of the students after classes also.

Where will the classes be conducted?

We will conduct the classes online so that the classes are available to anyone anywhere.

Can the course be done along with college?

Yes, we have arranged the class timings such that they don't hinder with your college schedule and don't affect your performance in the college. We will also provide preparatory holidays for your semester examinations. Our curriculum will also help you in performing better in computer-related subjects in your college.

At the end of this course, will you provide any course completion certificate?

Yes, every student who completes this course will receive a certificate from Placewit.

I am experienced. Can I still take this course?

Our course is designed to help every student in improving technical skills. We will start from the basics and quickly move to advanced topics so that you can see significant improvement in your skillset after completing this course, no matter where you stand today.

How do I get my doubts resolved?

Our instructors and mentors will be available after classes to resolve your doubts. You can also contact your instructors/mentors online if you need any help.

When does the course start?

We will announce the start date for next batch soon.

Program Plans

What are the program options?

We offer 3 programs. The first one includes access to our platform where you will find all the resources for placement preparation. The second one adds on live coding classes, doubt resolution, mock interviews and mentorship on top of that. The third plan also includes an extra month of revision, resume building, expert reviews, and mentor mock interviews. The prices are different for every instructor.

Can I apply for a fee waiver?

We have kept our introductory price very affordable for the students. But if you still have issues with that, please contact us on 9255410764. We will surely help you out.

Do I have to pay the fee in the beginning?

Since this course is an affordable one, we prefer if you pay the fee upfront. If you face any issues, we will surely resolve them. In case of major issues, we will refund your fee as well.
